Vote for the Xubuntu 26.04 Community Wallpapers
-----------------------------------------------

The voting period has opened for the [Xubuntu 26.04 Community Wallpaper Contest](https://discourse.ubuntu.com/t/xubuntu-26-04-community-wallpaper-contest/77213).

From February 18, 2026 through March 21, 2026, the Xubuntu community submitted 37 unique images to be considered for inclusion in the new long-term support (LTS) release of Xubuntu. We're finally ready to open up the community vote. From now through April 7, 2026, vote on your favorite images using the below Discourse link. Six winners will be announced and will receive Xubuntu stickers and [commemorative coins](https://floss.social/@pleia2/116205342058415682) by mail.

[Vote for your favorites!](https://discourse.ubuntu.com/t/vote-xubuntu-26-04-wallpaper-contest/79535)

What are you waiting for? Go vote!
