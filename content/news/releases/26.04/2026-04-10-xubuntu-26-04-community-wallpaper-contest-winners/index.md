The Xubuntu team is happy to announce the results of the 26.04 community wallpaper contest!

We'd like to send out a huge thanks to every contestant. The Xubuntu Community Wallpaper Contest gives us a unique chance to interact with the community and get contributions from members who may otherwise not have had the opportunity to join in before.

We received around 40 unique submissions, and 42 voters make 199 selections. The competition was fierce! There was a 5-way tie between 5th and 6th place, with the Xubuntu team making the final tie-breaking vote. All of the submissions are browsable on the [26.04 contest page at discourse.ubuntu.com](https://discourse.ubuntu.com/t/xubuntu-26-04-community-wallpaper-contest/77213/39).
